Fun facts about Animals

1. How to make a camel

  • Camels are domestic animals
  • Camels can go without water for 7 months
  • Camels live in hot locations

2. How to make a panda

  • Pandas eat bamboo
  • Pandas are endangered animals
  • Pandas have black and white fur

3. How to make a teddy

  • Teddy bears are fluffy
  • Teddy bears are brown, pink, white, and many other colors
  • Many children own a teddy bear

4. How to make a chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

5. How to make a pigeon

  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

6. How to make a cicada

  • Cicadas are like tiny violins
  • Cicadas make noise like toy frogs
  • Cicadas Live on All Continents Except Antarctica
